\section{Model 3: Mechanism Design}
\label{sec:model3}

We address: \textbf{Can we design a voting mechanism that improves fairness while maintaining engagement?}

\subsection{Operationalizing Fairness}

A \textbf{``wrongful elimination''} occurs when the eliminated contestant had \emph{higher} fan support than whoever would be eliminated under an alternative rule:
\begin{equation}
    E^{\text{actual}} \neq E^{\text{alt}} \quad \text{and} \quad \text{Rank}_{\text{fan}}(E^{\text{actual}}) > \text{Rank}_{\text{fan}}(E^{\text{alt}}).
\end{equation}

\subsection{Candidate Mechanisms}

\begin{enumerate}[itemsep=0.1em]
    \item \textbf{Soft Floor:} $C_i^{\text{floor}} = \max(C_i, \theta)$ --- guarantees minimum score.
    \item \textbf{Weighted Average:} $C_i = \alpha \widetilde{J}_i + (1-\alpha) \widetilde{V}_i$ --- adjustable judge weight.
    \item \textbf{Weighted Borda (Proposed):} Rank-based with $\alpha = 0.6$:
\end{enumerate}
\begin{equation}
    S_i^{\text{Borda}} = 0.6 \cdot \widetilde{J}_i + 0.4 \cdot \widetilde{V}_i,
\end{equation}
where $\widetilde{J}_i, \widetilde{V}_i$ are min-max normalized. Lowest score is eliminated.

\subsection{Counterfactual Simulation}

We replay all 230 elimination rounds under each mechanism:

\begin{table}[H]
    \centering
    \caption{Mechanism Comparison}
    \label{tab:mechanism_comparison}
    \begin{tabular}{lccc}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Mechanism} & \textbf{Param} & \textbf{Wrongful} & \textbf{Rate} \\
        \midrule
        Current System & -- & 40 & 17.4\% \\
        Soft Floor & $\theta=5\%$ & 94 & 40.9\% \\
        Weighted Avg & $\alpha=0.5$ & 28 & 12.2\% \\
        \textbf{Weighted Borda} & $\alpha=0.6$ & \textbf{3} & \textbf{1.3\%} \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
\end{table}

\noindent\textbf{Key Finding:} Soft Floor \emph{backfires} (94 wrongful); Weighted Borda achieves \textbf{92.5\% reduction}.

\subsection{The Safety Net Protocol}
\label{subsec:safety_net}

We propose a three-tiered mechanism:

\subsection{Implementation}

We recommend $\alpha = 0.6$ Weighted Borda: (1) 92.5\% fairness gain, (2) transparent ``60\% skill, 40\% fan,'' (3) preserved engagement, (4) robust to extreme values.
